Mencari nama pada buku tahunan

Jumlah halaman buku = 20
Nama yang ingin dicari "Vincent Julius"

Pseudo code ke -1 (Binary Search)

1. Read buku tahunan (20 Halaman)
2. Read to middle of buku tahunan (Halaman ke -10)
3. Look at the page
4. if "Vincent Julius" is on the page
5.     - Print "Vincent Julius ditemukan"
6. Else if "Vincent Julius" is earlier in buku tahunan 
7.     - Open to middle of left half of buku tahunan (Halaman ke-5)
8.     - Go back to line 3
9. Else if "Vincent Julius" is later in 
10.    - open to middle of right half of buku tahunan (Halaman ke-15)
11.    - Go back to line 3
12. Else 
13.    - Quit

Big O Notation at worst case = O(Log n)

Pseudo code ke-2 (Linear Search)

1. Read buku tahunan 
2. Open 2 page 
2. Find "Vincent Julius" from halaman 1 - 20
3. Quit

Big O Notation at worst case = O(n)
